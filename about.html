About Us

Who are we?
We are HOPEtech! Our name is an acronym for our names: Heewon, Oumou, Paula, and Elizabeth. We are participants of Girls Who Code which is a 7-week computer science course where we learn the fundamentals of computer science – from robotics to how to build a webpage – while gaining exposure to the tech industry.
Goal
As our final project, we wanted to address loneliness among people of an older generation, we realized that older people are especially vulnerable to loneliness and social isolation and it can have a serious effect on their health. But through the this website, senior citizens will be able to learn to navigate social media and video call interfaces.
